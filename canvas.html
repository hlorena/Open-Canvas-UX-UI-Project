Artist's Palette

Instructions

Use the creative prompt to draw the first image that comes to mind and try to focus on the task for a full minute. Use the timer to keep on-track and once you've reached a full 60 seconds of creative focus, you get a 5 star reward!


Want to start again? Press [DELETE/BACKSPACE] to clear your canvas and start over with a new prompt.


Looking to challenge your memory and add a little spontaneity to the drawing challenge? Press [TAB] to remove the creative prompt and change the canvas to an unexpected colour!


Want a copy of your artwork? To export your drawing click [Save Canvas] and download a copy to print or share on social media!


Tips

When using the pencil tool, make sure to keep the size at the smallest setting to avoid an emphasized starting point.


When using the stamp tools, change the colour and increase the size to see the outline of each shape.


Most importantly, have fun and let your creativity lead the way!
