Celebrating creative neurodiversity.

“Creativity is intelligence having fun”
- Albert Einstein


Did you know that ADHD also comes with neurological gifts? These unique abilities give individuals an increased capacity for hyper-stimulation and spontaneity. Some of these special traits have been used to describe creative masterminds like Einstein himself!


Open Canvas is a digital painting platform that gives ADHD users a space to unleash their creative potential in a playful environment designed especially for them.


Learn how to get started!

Use these coach marks to learn more about how you can tap into your creativity using Open Canvas.

Stimulating Environment

A colourful environment with different brush tools that encourage exploration and promote creative expression.

Task-based Prompts

Task-based creative prompts that help enhance memory and promote goal-directed art-based activities.

Positive Reinforcement

Emotional encouragment that foesters creativity, progress and promotes an enjoyable experience.
